The Technology Behind DNA Testing

Research By: Zion Bittle, Raven Taylor, Saber Jones, Emmanuel Sosa

Mentors: Susan Medlin, Colson Dunlap, Joel Moore

Abstract

DNA testing has, over the years, become more widely known and commercialized in the landscape of science and technology. What at one point was thought to be unachievable is now possible with the technology that powers Genetic DNA testing. However, it is important for consumers and students to have a grasp on what technology is used by major Genetic testing companies like Ancestory.com or 23andMe.com so that these consumers will understand what exactly they are submitting their DNA for. Our goal is to study the technology behind Genetic testing and compare and contrast that technology from different Genetic Testing services. Furthermore, we hypothesize that Technology plays a crucial role in the operations that power Genetic DNA Testing
